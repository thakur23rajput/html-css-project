Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, and baking soda in a large bowl.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat for 2 minutes on medium speed.
  4. Pour batter evenly into prepared pans.
  5. Bake 30-35 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean.
  6. Cool for 10 minutes, then remove from pans to wire racks. Cool completely.
